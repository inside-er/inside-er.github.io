Legal
PRIVACY &
DATA.
1. NO DATA FOR SALE

Your identity, your campus activity, and your private connections are yours alone. INSIDE will never sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties. Our business model is based on premium subscriptions — not on selling your privacy.

2. CAMPUS VERIFICATION

Every user is verified via their college email before gaining access to a campus feed. This ensures a closed, safe ecosystem for students. We periodically re-verify status to ensure the network remains campus-only.

3. ANONYMITY CONTROLS

When you post anonymously, your identity is cryptographically shielded. No other user — including moderators — can see your real name. In cases of extreme legal violations or threats to campus safety, we retain the ability to identify the user for legal compliance, but this is governed by strict internal protocols.

4. DATA SECURITY

We employ industry-standard encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Your authentication details are handled using secure, hashed protocols. Our servers are located in secure regions compliant with Indian data protection regulations.

5. AUTOKRYX OVERVIEW

INSIDE is operated by Autokryx Technologies Private Limited. All data processing is governed by our singular philosophy of building secure, private, human-centric digital infrastructure for the next generation of India.
